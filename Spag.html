Level Amateur
Preparation time 0 hr
Cooking Time 30 minutes
Yield 1 serving

Ingredients:

  1. Half a pound of pasta
  2. 4 cloves of garlic, thinnly sliced.
  3. Half a cup of olive oil
  4. Quater teaspoon of red pepper chilli flakes.
  5. freshly ground black-pepper to taste
  6. Half a cup of freshly chopped parsley
  7. A cup of grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese or your favourite cheese

Steps:

Step 1:Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add salt to season. Spaghetti goes in. Stir occasionally to avoid burning the spaghetti. After about 11 mins, taste it for texture and doneness . If done, drain in a pasta bowl.

Step 2:Add the chopped garlic and oil to a cold skilled to avoid burning the oil. Fry the garlic until golden brown. Keep the heat on low for about five minutes, then turn of the gas.

Step 3: Add salt, pepper and red chilli pepper flakes. Add the oil and garlic to the pot. Add half the cheese. Stir until well combined.

Step 4: Serve the pasta with the remaining cheese and enjoy!

History of Spaghetti aglio e olio

  • Traditional dish from Naples, Italy.
  • Although chesse is not part of the tradional recipe, it is more a matter of want rather than keeping tradition.
  • Result of tradional italian cooking, making as much as possible with the fewest ingredients
